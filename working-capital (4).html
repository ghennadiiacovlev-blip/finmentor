Treasury

Treasury: payment calendar and priorities

When there is not enough cash for every payment, order matters. Treasury turns a chaotic payment list into a process with priorities and liquidity control.

Payment calendar

All obligations in one place, with due dates and funding sources. The owner sees what to pay now, what to defer and what needs approval.

Priorities and funds

Payments are split by priority (critical, operating, growth), and funds reserve money for management purposes.

7 / 14 / 30-day coverage

Coverage control surfaces the cash gap early and reduces reactive decisions.

FAQ

Does treasury replace accounting?

No. It is a management cash-control layer, separate from bookkeeping.

Where do we start?

With the payment calendar and payment prioritisation.

Bring order to payments

A Discovery Call or treasury implementation within the Business Control System.
