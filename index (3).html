Working capital quick scan

Answer a few questions to understand where money may get stuck in the business: in receivables, inventory, advances, payment terms or payment discipline.

7 short questions. The result appears right on the page — no sign-up and no automatic data sending.

Question 1 / 7

Do you have profit in reports yet regularly run short of cash?

Question 2 / 7

Do clients delay payment beyond agreed terms?

Question 3 / 7

Do you know how much money is locked in inventory / stock / work in progress?

Question 4 / 7

Do you have a payment calendar for the next 30 days?

Question 5 / 7

Can you see a cash gap 7 / 14 / 30 days in advance?

Question 6 / 7

Are there rules for which payments are critical and which can be deferred?

Question 7 / 7

How are financial decisions made now?
