Privacy Policy

Updated: 22 June 2026

This Policy describes what data FINMENTOR (the external-CFO practice of Ghennadi Iacovlev) processes, for what purpose and what rights you have. It reflects the current site structure: web analytics, the working capital quick scan, the diagnostic questionnaire, the financial navigator and contact channels (Telegram, WhatsApp, email).

1. Who processes the data

Data controller — Ghennadi Iacovlev (FINMENTOR), Chișinău, Republic of Moldova. For enquiries: cfo@finmentor.md.

2. What data we collect

The data you provide in forms and messages (name, contact, company, comment); questionnaire answers; quick-scan answers are processed in your browser, and optional fields are used only if you fill in and send them; messages via Telegram/WhatsApp/email; technical web-analytics data.

3. The financial navigator

The navigator works on predefined rules (rule-based), does not collect personal data and does not give individual financial advice. Only anonymous interaction events may reach web analytics.

4. Web analytics with no personal data

The site uses Google Analytics 4. We do not send personal data (name, email, phone, company, comments) to web analytics. From the quick scan, only the general result level (low / medium / high risk) is sent.

5. Purposes of processing

To contact you and hold a conversation based on your request; to prepare a proposal; to improve the site based on anonymous analytics.

6. Legal basis

Processing is based on your consent, which can be withdrawn at any time.

7. Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes above, then delete it. At your request, we delete it earlier.

8. Providers and automation

We do not sell your data. Trusted providers may be involved (GitHub Pages hosting, Cloudflare, Google services, email). In the future, request intake may be partly automated (e.g. Make / n8n, Google Sheets, email notifications) — only for the purposes described and with your consent.

9. Remote work and NDA

FINMENTOR can work remotely. Client data is used only for agreed tasks. An NDA is signed on request; access is minimal; final files and systems stay with the client.

10. Your rights

To obtain information about your data, correct it, delete it and withdraw consent. Write to cfo@finmentor.md.

11. Changes

We may update this Policy. The current version is always available on this page with the update date.

This document is general and informational and is not legal advice. Before public use it must be reviewed by a lawyer and brought into compliance with the legislation of the Republic of Moldova and (for EU clients) the GDPR, with the operator's actual details filled in.
