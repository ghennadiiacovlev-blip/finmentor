Working capital: where the money gets stuck

A business can show profit yet have no free cash if cash flow is stuck in receivables, inventory, advances or payment terms.

Where the money gets stuck

The most common areas: overdue receivables, slow-moving inventory, advances and unbalanced payment terms.

Cash conversion cycle

We link receivables, payables and inventory to see in how many days money returns to the account — and where the cycle can be shortened.

Link to Cash Flow

Working capital connects to the payment calendar and the liquidity forecast, so freeing up cash becomes a regular control.
